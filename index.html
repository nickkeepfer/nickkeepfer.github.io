About me

I'm interested in Quantum Fluid Dynamics and Machine Learning. I obtained my co-tutelle PhD from the University of Newcastle and the University of Trento in 2022. Now, I am working as a Postdoctoral Research Associate at Newcastle University. Here I am working on projects ranging from Quantum Droplet Quench Dynamics, Machine Learning, to Fuzzy Dark Matter. Outside of work, you might find me rock-climbing, drinking coffee or doing some Jiu-Jitsu.
